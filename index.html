Hello! I AM

I was a blogger and vlogger. My life was a collection of stories, captured in pixels and words. I thrived in a team, adapted under pressure, and upheld the professionalism demanded by every institution I was part of. But beyond deadlines and expectations, I lived for art, for the thrill of flavors, for the endless roads stretching beyond the horizon. Nature was my sanctuary, my escape. However, there was another side of me that few people knew. I was obsessed with the shadows—espionage, hacking, and digital crime. I studied infiltration techniques, encryption, and how a person’s digital footprint could be erased or traced. It was just an interest, a hobby... or so I thought. Every adventure, every secret, every fleeting moment—I documented them all. My social media held my history. My past. My truth. But now… someone is watching. Someone who knows my every move. They aren’t just observing; they’re leaving traces—subtle, almost invisible, but enough to send a chill down my spine. I don’t know who they are. I don’t know what they want. But I do know one thing: I will limit the information I share, for there is always a deceiver disguised as a listener, and always a listener who hides in disguise.
